🍪 Cookies Policy

We use cookies to make your experience on Ivory Dough Symphony as warm and delightful as a fresh-baked loaf. This policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and how you can manage them.

Last updated: July 10, 2026

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites place on your device (computer, tablet, or phone) to store information about your preferences, settings, and browsing activity. They help us remember you and improve your experience.

2. How we use cookies

We use cookies for several purposes:

  • Essential cookies: These are necessary for the website to function properly (e.g., navigation, form submissions).
  • Preference cookies: They remember your choices, such as language or region, so we can provide a more personalized experience.
  • Analytics cookies: These help us understand how visitors interact with our site — which pages are popular, how long you stay, and how you found us. We use this data to improve our content and design.
  • Marketing cookies: We may use these (with your consent) to deliver relevant content or offers based on your interests.

3. Types of cookies we use

  • Session cookies: Temporary and deleted when you close your browser.
  • Persistent cookies: Remain on your device for a set period (e.g., 30 days) to remember your preferences.
  • First-party cookies: Set directly by us (Ivory Dough Symphony).
  • Third-party cookies: Set by external services we integrate (e.g., Google Analytics for traffic analysis).

4. How to manage cookies

You have control over cookies. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View and delete individual cookies.
  • Block cookies from specific sites.
  • Set preferences for all cookies.

To manage cookies, go to your browser settings (usually under "Privacy" or "Site Settings"). Please note that disabling essential cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

5. Consent and changes

When you first visit our site, we ask for your consent to use non-essential cookies. You can change your preferences at any time by adjusting your browser settings or clicking the cookie preference link in our footer.

We may update this policy occasionally — we'll post the revised version here with a new "Last updated" date.

6. Contact us

If you have questions about our cookies policy, please email us at hello@ivorydough.com or use our contact form.
